Day 1 - Data Collection 

The dataset required for this project is simple: a subject and their heartbeat. Acquiring this is slightly more complicated. The following procedure must be executed for an accurate and consistent data collection:

Background: Solid white wall or background to reduce color interference for the Kinect RGB Camera.  

Setup: LED Light facing subject in white shirt (again, to reduce color interference). All room lights off to get rid of flickering in individual frames. BP band around arm, and HR sensor on finger. HR screen taped to wall or placed on object near subject, tilted away from subject slightly as to avoid any reflection onto the subject to reduce the chances of any computer cheating.

Test 1

Objective: gather multiple inputs of each subject's regular, at rest, heart rate. Subject in white shirt looks directly into camera for 1 minute.

**Multiple trials per subject

Test 2

Objective: collect data with an increase in the subject's heart rate (i.e. exercise). Subject, still in white shirt, leaves room and performs a series of blood-pumping tasks like running up and down stairs, around the hallway, or campus. They then return to the setup room and looks directly into camera for 1 minute.  

**Multiple Trials per subject

  1. Rest - Test 1
  2. Exercise 1 - Simple Test 2. Stimulate heart
  3. Exercise 2 - Harder Test 2. Peak of heart rate. 
  4. Rest - Test 1. Heart rate returning back to normal. 
  5. Rest - Should be close in value to Test 1
